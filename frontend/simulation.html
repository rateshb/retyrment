‚ÑπÔ∏è Understanding Monte Carlo Simulation

What is it? Monte Carlo simulation runs thousands of scenarios with random market returns to show the range of possible outcomes for your portfolio.

How to read: The percentiles show different probability levels. For example, the 50th percentile (median) means 50% of simulations resulted in this value or higher.

Use it for: Understanding risk, planning for different market conditions, and making informed investment decisions.
