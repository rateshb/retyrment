## Retyrment: Your All‑in‑One Personal Finance & Retirement Platform

Retyrment helps individuals and families take control of their financial future with a unified, data‑driven planning experience — from daily cash‑flow to long‑term retirement readiness.

### Why Retyrment
- **All‑in‑one money hub**: Track income, expenses, investments, loans, insurance, goals, and family profiles in one place.
- **Actionable insights**: Turn your data into clear next steps, not just charts.
- **Retirement‑ready planning**: Build confidence with projections, gap analysis, and income strategies.

---

## Key Features

### Financial Tracking
- **Income Tracking**: Multiple sources, frequency, status, and growth assumptions.
- **Expense Management**: Fixed/variable, time‑bound, recurring, category insights.
- **Investments Portfolio**: MF, stocks, FD/RD, PPF/EPF, NPS, gold, crypto, cash, real estate.
- **Loan Management**: EMIs, tenure, amortization, and post‑loan investment opportunities.
- **Insurance Management**: Health, term, ULIP, endowment, money‑back, vehicle, pension.
- **Family Profiles**: Dependents, ages, and coverage context.

### Retirement Planning Engine
- **Year‑by‑year retirement matrix**
- **Corpus & gap analysis**
- **SIP step‑up modeling**
- **Maturing investment inflows**
- **Strategy planning** (reinvest, sell illiquid assets, optimize SIPs)
- **Withdrawal strategies** (Sustainable / 4% Rule / Depletion)

### Smart Analysis & Insights
- **Net worth dashboard**
- **Goal funding status**
- **Personalized recommendations**
- **Emergency fund readiness**
- **Insurance adequacy assessment**
- **Monte Carlo simulation** for probabilistic outcomes

### Reports & Export
- **PDF reports** (Financial summary, Retirement report, Calendar report)
- **Excel retirement matrix**
- **JSON backup/export**

### Admin & Access Control
- Role‑based access (FREE / PRO / ADMIN)
- Feature toggles per user
- Trial management and role expiry automation
- User and feature governance
